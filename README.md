# 🕸️ ARC-IIA: Autonomous Risk Intelligence AI

> **The Digital "Spider-Sense" for Banking Inspection.**

**ARC-IIA** (Autonomous Risk Control Intelligence Investigation Assistant) is a cutting-edge AI-driven system designed to empower banking inspection officers. It proactively identifies, analyzes, and mitigates financial crimes using real-time behavioral telemetry and graph-based relationship scoring.

---

## 🚀 The Vision

In an era of rapid digital transactions, traditional fraud detection is too slow. **ARC-IIA** acts as an autonomous layer of intelligence that "feels" domestic and international threats before they manifest into major financial losses.

## 🛠️ Key Detection Capabilities

The system is built to identify 10 critical risk patterns:

1. **Geo-Velocity Fraud:** Detects "impossible travel" (e.g., Mumbai to Delhi in 20 minutes).
2. **Device Takeover:** Flags high-value transactions coinciding with new SIMs, IPs, or devices.
3. **Dormant Account Activation:** Monitors sudden large transfers from long-inactive accounts.
4. **Account Draining:** Identifies rapid micro-transactions intended to empty a balance.
5. **Mule Account Networks:** Uses graph-based scoring to find links to known suspicious nodes.
6. **Merchant Category Shift:** Flags incongruent spending (e.g., a student suddenly purchasing industrial chemicals).
7. **Behavioral Time Deviation:** Detects transactions occurring outside a user's historical time window.
8. **Transaction Splitting:** Identifies "smurfing" patterns (splitting large amounts into tiny transfers).
9. **Failed Login Burst:** Monitors brute-force attempts followed by successful high-value transfers.
10. **International IP Anomalies:** Flags domestic profiles suddenly accessing via foreign VPNs.

## 🏗️ System Architecture

The platform operates across three integrated tiers:

* **User & Access Tier:** Secure portal for Investigation Officers to review flagged cases.
* **Application Services Tier:** Handles cheque processing, image extraction, and real-time data validation.
* **AI & Risk Tier:** The core engine that performs behavioral reasoning and generates the final **ARC Risk Score**.
